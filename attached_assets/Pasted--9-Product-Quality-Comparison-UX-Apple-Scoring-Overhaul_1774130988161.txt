#9 - Product Quality, Comparison UX & Apple Scoring Overhaul

What & Why
This is a focused upgrade to improve trust, clarity, and usability across product results and comparison in THA.

The goals are:
- make product cards more informative and trustworthy
- ensure result quality (correct language, clear ingredient data)
- introduce multi-product comparison (2–4 items)
- replace the current apple scoring system with a simpler additive-based model

This should be implemented cleanly without drifting into unrelated areas.

---

Done looks like

Product cards
- show concise ingredient summary
- clearly indicate when ingredient detail is incomplete
- display retailer/shop
- display apple rating (new additive-based score)

Result quality
- only return products in the same language as the user
- no foreign-language products in standard results

Comparison UX
- user can select 2–4 products
- "Compare" button appears when ≥2 selected
- comparison opens in a centered modal (NOT slide-over)
- products displayed side-by-side

Scoring
- apple rating is based purely on additive count
- simple, consistent, explainable

---

Core rules

1. ingredient visibility must be clear and concise
2. missing ingredient data must be clearly disclosed
3. retailer must be visible on product cards
4. results must match user language
5. comparison must support 2–4 products
6. modal must be centered (not a right slide panel)
7. apple score must be based on additive count only

---

Out of scope

- homepage/auth/demo/onboarding work
- full UI redesign
- heavy animation libraries
- unrelated backend refactors

T010 — Introduce additive classification layer

  - preservatives

  - flavourings

- additiveCodes (E-numbers)

---

T011 — Replace apple rating system (additive-based)

Remove existing scoring logic and replace with:

- 5 apples = 0 additives
- 4 apples = 1 additive
- 3 apples = 2–3 additives
- 2 apples = 4 additives
- 1 apple = 5+ additives

Rules:
- do NOT penalise NOVA 3
- do NOT use NOVA as scoring driver
- scoring based purely on additive count
