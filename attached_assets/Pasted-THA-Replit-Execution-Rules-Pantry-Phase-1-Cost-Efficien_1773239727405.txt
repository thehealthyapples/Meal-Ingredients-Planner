Pantry merge rules

Apply simple, low-risk merge behaviour in the basket display only.

Merge when:
• same pantry product family
• compatible variant/type

Do NOT merge when:
• format or subtype materially changes

Examples:
• olive oil + olive oil = merge
• olive oil + olive oil spray = do NOT merge
• chopped tomatoes + tinned chopped tomatoes = merge if your current normalisation already treats these as same base pantry item
• tomato puree + chopped tomatoes = do NOT merge

Implementation guidance:
Use a simple merge key based on normalized pantry item name.
Strip harmless packaging/container words if already easy to do.
Do NOT over-engineer fuzzy merging in this phase.
